Penjelasan dengan langkah-langkah:

Berpelurus (180°), sehingga :

2x + 10° + x + 8° = 180°

3x + 18° = 180°

3x = 180° - 18°

3x = 162°

x = 162°/3°

x = 54°

12. 1. I was sleeping when somebody knocked the door.A. I was slept when somebody knocked the door.B. Thad slept when somebody knocked the door.C. T have slept when somebody knocked the door.D. I have slept when somebody knock the door.Pakai rumus Past perfect​


Jawaban:

B. I had slept when somebody knocked the door.

Pembahasan

Pola Past Perfect:

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

(-) S + had not + V3 + Complement

(?) had + S + V3 + Complement

Keterangan pilihan ganda:

1. Past Continuous

(+) S + Tobe (was/were) + V-ing + O

=> I was sleeping when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

Opsi ini salah bukan Past Perfect

A. Was + Verb 2

=> I was slept when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

Was + V2 = salah

B. Past Perfect

(+) S + had + V3 + Complement

=> I had slept when somebody knocked the door.

Penjelasan:

C dan D. Present Perfect

(+) S + has/have + V3 + Complement

C. I have slept

D. I have slept

Penjelasan:

Ini kalimat Present Perfect, sedangkan yg diminta di soal adalah Past Perfect

Kesimpulan

Yang sesuai pola Past Perfect adalah B.

22. somebody help me ,please!!!


Dik:
waktu lokal kota A= 09.00
waktu lokal kota B= 12.40
posisi kota A= 105°BB-5°LS

Dit:
perbedaan waktu= ?
posisi kota B= ?

Untuk mengetahui perbedaan waktu kedua kota, kurangkan waktu kota B dengan kota A:

12.40 - 09.00= 3 jam 40 menit
[tex] = 3 \frac{40}{60} jam \\ \\ = 3 \frac{2}{3} jam[/tex]
1 jam = 15°

Untuk mengetahui koordinat kota B, kalikan selisih waktu (satuan jam) dengan 15°

[tex] = 3 \frac{2}{3} \times 15 \\ = \frac{11}{3} \times 15 \\ = 55[/tex]
Karena koordinat kota A terletak di bujur barat, maka kurangkan koordinat kota A dengan hasil tadi:

105° - 55°
= 50°BB

Jadi, jawaban yang paling tepat adalah A.50°BB-10°LU
