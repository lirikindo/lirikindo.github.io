29. mari belajar Kawan.....​


Jawaban:

I must do it at once : It must be done at once by me.

I can't send of the letter today : The letter cannot be send today.

Penjelasan:

Kalimat kedua seharusnya : "I can't send the letter today" kata "of" tidak diperlukan dalam kalimat tersebut.

Jawaban:

✅ It must be done by me at once

✅ the letter can not be sent by me today

PEMBAHASAN

• Berdasarkan Rumus, Active:

(+) S + modal (must/can) + Verb 1 + O

1. I must do it at once

(Saya harus MENGERJAKAN itu sekaligus)

(-) S + modal (must/can) + not + V1 + O

2. I cant send of the letter today

(Saya tidak bisa MENGIRIM surat itu hari ini)

• Maka Berdasarkan Rumus Passive:

(+) S + modal (must/can) + Be + V3 + By

1. it must be done by me at once
