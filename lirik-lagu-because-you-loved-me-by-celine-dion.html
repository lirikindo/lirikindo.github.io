Here are the answer for those song-based questions above.

1. Beyonce was once with Destiny's Child.

2. Taylor Swift sang a single entitled 'Safe and Sound' for the first movie of Hunger Games saga.

3. The movie was called 'Up Close and Personal'

Penjelasan:

Here are the elaboration for the answers above:

Back in late 90s and early 2000s, Destiny's Child was a famous girlband and Beyonce was always the most attractive one in the band. Along with Kelly Rowland and Michelle Williams, they rocked the world of pop music with their energetic performance in RnB genre and even won Grammy Awards too. Their notable singles were 'Survivor', 'Independent Woman', 'Emotion' and 'Bootylicious'.Taylor Swift made the song back in 2011 and collaborated with Civil Wars band for 'Safe and Sound'. Unfortunately, the song was not as succesful as the movie itself.Celine Dion performed the OST of Up Close and Personal which was released in 1996. Starred by Robert Redford and Michelle Pfeiffer, the movie was less impressive than the song which was nominated for Oscar as the best Soundtrack in that year.

Than the lyrics "that is how I know you go on" means that although a person dies their heart lives on. Than the lyrics "near far wherever you are I believe that the heart does go on" she does not know where his heart is but she knows it lives on, most likely referring to heaven. Than "we'll stay forever this way you are safe in my heart" means no matter what her love for him will never change and that his memory and the memory of their love is safe in her heart.

"A New Day Has Come" is a song by Canadian recording artist Celine Dion for her seventh English-language album of the same name (2002). The song was written by Aldo Nova and Stephan Moccio and produced by Walter Afanasieff and Nova. It was released as the album's lead single on 11 March 2002.
