15. Change the direct speech into indirect speech! 1. Niji says, "I see your son performing at Bulan Bahasa Festival." Answer: 2. They say, "We will spend our holiday in Bunaken." Answer: 3. Hanif said, "1 didn't understand what Mr. Sylvester say. Answer: 4. I told him, "Get out from my house." Answer: 5. She said, "I wish that I could do like the cool kids." Answer:


DIRECT-REPORTED SPEECH

1. Niji says (that) he sees my son performing at Bulan Bahasa Festival.

2. They say (that) they will spend their holiday in Bunaken.

3. Hanif said (that) he hadn't understood what Mr. Sylvester said.

4. I ordered him to get out from my house.

5. She said she wished that she could do like the cool kids.

EXPLANATION

Reported speech (indirect speech) adalah pernyataan pelaporan ulang dari apa yang telah disampaikan oleh narasumber kepada orang lain tanpa mengubah informasi yg tersirat. Pada umumnya, quotation mark ["....."] pada direct speech akan dihilangkan jila ingin dirubah ke dalam bentuk reported speech.

Perubahan bentuk noun clause verb pada reported speech bergantung pada bentuk reporting verb & jenis informasi yg disampaikan pada direct speech, apakah berupa informasi faktual, perintah, kalimat tanya dan larangan.

Jika reporting verb berbentuk present/future {says, will ask, tells}, noun clause verb tidak mengalami backshift.

Exp.

D.S : Marie says, "I CAN help you if there's enough time."

R.S : Marie says (that) SHE CAN help me if there's enough time.

Yang berubah pada noun clause-nya adalah pronominanya dan bentuk verb tense masih tetap.

Sedangkan, jika reporting verb berbentuk past {said, told, asked}, noun clause verb mengalami backshift baik dari keterangan waktu, petunjuk maupun tenses.

Simple present ➛Simple pastSimple past ➛Past perfectPresent perfect ➛Past perfectPast continuous ➛Past perfect continuousNow ➛at that timeToday ➛ that dayYesterday ➛ the day beforeTomorrow ➛ the next dayLast week ➛ the previous week

Exp.

D.S : Catherine said, "I AM sick now."

R.S : Catherine said (that) SHE WAS sick at that time."

Dikarenakan menggunakan reporting verb "said", ada perubahan tenses pada noun clause verb-nya, di mana yg awalnya simple present menjadi simple past. Kemudian, pronomina juga berubah. Yang awalnya menggunakan kata ganti orang pertama "I" berubah menjadi kata ganti orang ketiga tunggal "She." Kemudian, keterangan waktu "now" ikut berubah menjadi "at that time." Waktunya jadi mundur.
