5. Apasih Arti "Were Gonna Die Young" Dan "Let's Make The Most To The Night Like Were Gonna Die young"


We're gonna die young
Kita akan mati muda
let's make most to the night like we're gonna die young bisa dimaknai dengan
"mari nikmati malam ini seolah esok kita akan mati"
Were Gonna Die Young = Kita akan mati muda
Let's Make The Most To The Night Like Were Gonna Die young = Ayo buat kenang kenangan atau melakukan sesuatu yang kita sukai malam ini, seperti kita akan mati
