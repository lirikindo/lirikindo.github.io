5. buatlah text narrative tentang sleeping beauty or snow white


Long time ago, in a faraway land. King Stevan and his queen wished for a child. Finally a daughter was born and they named her Aurora. To honor the baby princess the king help a great feast. He also invited his good friend king Hubert to the feast. King Hubert had brought his young son Philip. The kings agreed that someday Philip and Aurora would be married.
Among the guest were three good fairies, Flora, Fauna, and Marryweather. They wished to bless the princess with a give. Waving her wand, Flora said, “ My gift shall be the gift of beauty.” “ And mine.” Said Fauna, “ Shall be the gift of song.”, Marryweather turn was next. But before the Marryweather could speak the castle door flew opened. It was the evil witch named Maleficent. She was furious because she had not been to the feast. “ My gift for the child is, before the sun sets on her sixteenth birthday she shall prick her finger on the spindle of a spinning wheel and die!” , Than she disappeared.
But Marryweather still had gift to give, so she said, “ She will not die but she will fall into a deep sleep and when theirs true love’s kiss the spell shall break and the princess opened her eyes!”King Stevan feared the witch curse so the good fairies hatched a plan. They would take Aurora to live with them, deep in the woods, saved from Maleficent. The king and queen agreed with heavy heart.
To guard their secret, the fairies changed Aurora’s named to Briar Rose. The years passed quietly, and Briar Rose grew into a beautiful young woman. At last the princess reached her sixteenth birthday, in a mossy glen she danced and sang with her friends, the birds and animals. She told them of her beautiful dream about meeting a tall. Handsome boy and falling in love.A handsome boy came. When he heard Briar Rose singing he jumped for his horse and reached out to take her hand. Briar Rose was startled. “ I do not mean to frighten you, but I feel like we have met before.” Said the youngman. Briar Rose felt very happy. But she didn’t know that he was prince Phillip and Prince Phillip didn’t know that she was Princess Aurora.
Back at the cottage, Briar Rose told the fairies that she had falling in love. “ impossible! “ cried the fairies. Then, they told her that she was a royal princess. It was time to return home. Actually the witch Maleficent knew that Aurora was return home. Using her evil powers she lured Aurora to high tower in the tiny room spinning well suddenly appeared. “ Touch the spindle, touch it.''said the witch. The three good fairies rushed to the rescue, but they were too late. Aurora touched the sharp spindle and felt into deep sleep.
Meanwhile, the witch captured Phillip and chain him deep on her dungeon. But the good fairies had other plans for him. They melted the prince’s chain. And they armed him with the shield of virtue and the sword of truth. Than they sent him to the castle to awaken the princess.When the witch saw Philip escaping she was changed into a Monstrous Dragon to stop him. Prince Phillip threw his sword to sword the witch. Then Maleficent was no more. Then Prince Phillip raced to the tower where his love lay sleeping. Gently he kissed her and Aurora eyes slowly opened. The kings and queens planed for the prince and princess wedding. And they lived happily.

15. Majas dari lagu Beautiful In White​


"Majas" adalah istilah yang merujuk pada penggunaan gaya bahasa atau ekspresi artistik yang kreatif dalam bahasa untuk memberikan efek retoris atau figuratif. Namun, lagu "Beautiful In White" tidak secara khusus menggunakan majas dalam liriknya. Lirik lagu tersebut lebih bersifat deskriptif dan mengungkapkan perasaan cinta dan keindahan.

Lirik lagu "Beautiful In White" oleh Shane Filan menggambarkan rasa cinta dan keindahan seseorang yang diungkapkan oleh penyanyi. Ia menggambarkan perasaannya terhadap seseorang yang sangat dia cintai dan menganggapnya indah dalam balutan putih. Lirik-lirik tersebut menggunakan bahasa yang sederhana dan lugas, tanpa penggunaan majas yang khas.

Namun, terdapat beberapa istilah dan perbandingan yang dapat dianggap sebagai majas secara umum. Misalnya, penyanyi mengungkapkan perasaan dengan mengatakan "You look so beautiful in white" yang dapat dianggap sebagai perbandingan (simile) untuk menyampaikan betapa indahnya orang yang dimaksud dengan warna putih.

Jadi, meskipun tidak terdapat majas yang terlihat secara khusus dalam lagu "Beautiful In White", lagu ini masih dapat menggambarkan perasaan cinta dan keindahan dengan cara yang sederhana dan langsung melalui lirik-liriknya.

21. Nada dasar apa yang digunkan dalam lagu beautiful in white


Jawaban:

Nada dasar lagu ini adalah C mayor, diikuti Overtune ke D mayor pada reff ke sekian, dan overtune sekali lagi ke E mayor setelah bridge. Jadi, lagu ini hanya ditulis dalam 1 versi ya. Jangan lupa, kalau sudah overtune, melodi tangan kanan mengikuti tangga nada baru nya. Jangan sampai chord sudah berubah, tapi melodi masih mengikuti tangga nada C. Pastinya bunyinya akan fales sana sini.
