27. Mention name of the days in a week !. tolong sama beri artinya


In English, the name of days in a week is quite straightforward and simple. So does its translation. It should not be too difficult to follow.

Penjelasan:

Here's the name of days plus its translation, which is actually taken from the names of the order of planets in Hellenistic astrology.

Sunday (hari minggu) is inspired from the day of the Sun or Helios. It means the first day.Monday (hari senin) is also called the day of the moon. Tuesday (hari selasa) is inspired from the day of Mars.Wednesday (hari rabu) is called the day of Mercury.Thursday (hari kamis) is inspired from the day of Jupiter or Jove. Friday (hari jumat) is called the day of Venus.Saturday (hari sabtu) is the last day and inspired from the day of Saturn.
